Week 5 | Vinyl Printing and Cutting


The task for the week was to create an enclosure for Raspberry Pi or Ardunio Uno using 360 Fusion design program.



Vinyl Graphic Stickers


I selected a few images off the internet and uploaded them to Coreldraw. I edited the size of my pictures and changed the resolutions. To make sure the machine (Roland GS-24) recognizes the lines, I added contour to the pictures. The lines of the contour had to be a designated pink color. The machine first prints the stickers and goes back to cut the stickers. One of the stickers made it to the collection on my laptop! Congratulations!




Contour Stickers


Also, using Coreldraw, I edited an image of black-white monkey and uploaded to the computer next to the CAMM 1 GS-24 machine. I am so excited about how my stickers turned out to be! I am planning to make more when I have time to put them on my laptop, binders, etc.
